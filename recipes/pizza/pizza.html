Pizza

Italian pizza with mozzarella and tomatoe sauce

This delicious recipe will yield you the best pizza you can find on the internet. It's fluffy, tangy and above all delicious as fuck!

To follow the recipe, you will need to have access to an oven, preferably with a pizza stone, some basic ingredients and 30 minutes of your time.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Dry yeast
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil (optional

Steps

  1. Mix 500g of flour and dry yeast with 300ml water until smooth
  2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes and increase in size
  3. Once risen, roll into a pizza shape
  4. Add tomatoe sauce and mozzarella (basil optional)
  5. Place into an already heated oven for 10 minutes
  6. When baked, take out and enjoy!
